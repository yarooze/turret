Rules

  1. Factories brings you resources (gems)
  2. Destroying meteorites bring you resources (gems)
  3. Shooting costs you resources (gems)
  4. Meteorites can destroy your factories
  5. Meteorites can destroy the turret
  6. Your bullet can destroy your factories
  7. Your bullet can destroy the turret
  8. Meteorites can destroy the turret
  9. The turret has some lives (hearts)
  10. If you have no resources (gems) - you lose
  11. If you have no lives (hearts) - you lose
